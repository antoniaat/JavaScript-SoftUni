My name is Elizabeth Taylor, and I’m a freshman studying Software Engineering in Software University in the beautiful Sofia, Bulgaria. I love to solve problems. Whether it’s finding the most elegant way to write a line of code or figuring out which chord fits best into a progression, I love the challenge of finding a way and discovering solutions. Maybe that’s why I love writing and the satisfaction that comes from finding just the right word; or why I study psychology and rationality in my spare time to figure out how people think. As long as there’s a problem to solve or a challenge to puzzle over, it’s bound to be something I love!
